Hope for the Holidays!

Every year Hope 4 You organizes, with the help of donors and sponsors, hope for the holiday season for local families. This year, with the help of our fantastic sponsors, over 120 kids will have a special holiday season!

FREE Food Giveaways

With our great local partnerships, we've had the to opportunity to provide free food giveaways for local families in the area. This year we surpassed 60,000+ pounds of food, that's 30 tons! We wouldn't be able to do this without a great group of volunteers, thank you!
